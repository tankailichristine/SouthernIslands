Get to know the different Islands
The Southern Islands consists of eight islands off Singapore’s south coast: St John Island, Lazarus Island, Seringat Island, Kusu Island, Big Sister’s Island, Little Sister’s Island, Tekukor Island and Sentosa.

The more popular islands that most people visit for their day trips are St John Island, Lazarus Island, Seringat Island, and Kusu Island.


Main Islands

St John Island

St. John’s Island is the largest of the southern islands. It's rustic vibes provides a perfect place to escape from the city life in Singapore. Chalets are also available for booking!

Things to do

Picnic

An island getaway wouldn’t be complete without the quintessential al fresco dining. Surround yourself in the beauty of nature as you much on your sandwiches!

Swim

Take a dip in the clear blue waters of Larazus Island and capture the most Instagrammable photos of its mesmerizing white sandy beach that is shaped like a crescent.

Rent a bike and explore the islands

With St. John's and Lazarus Islands being connected by a bridge, the best way to explore the islands' breathtaking nature and wildlife is on a bike, and you can get your rental bicycle from St. John's Island itself!

Travel Tips

1) Stock up on water, food and sunblock

There are no shops on the islands, so do bring along your own supplies or buy them from the small shop at Marina South Pier to last the day!

2) Check the ferry schedule ahead of time

Be sure to know what time the ferries arrive and leave the islands so that you can plan your day around them. The ferries may not always depart at their scheduled timings so be sure to be at the jetty on early!

3) Bring tissues

There are three public toilets on St John Island, one on Seringat Island, and one on Kusu Island. Most of them do not provide toilet paper so be sure to bring your own!

4) Have ample rest

You will be out walking under the sun the whole day so make sure you get enough rest the night before! You may also want to bring an umbrella or cap.
